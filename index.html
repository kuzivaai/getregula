EU AI Act Article 2 is extraterritorial: if your app has users in the EU, the rules apply regardless of where you are based. Most chatbots and productivity tools are limited-risk — one transparency disclosure required, nothing more. Run Regula to confirm your tier and get the exact obligations.

What the law covers — and what Regula checks

Not just a questionnaire — Regula reads your code directly and maps it to the regulation.

Banned uses

Is your app prohibited?

Social scoring, subliminal manipulation, real-time facial recognition, emotion inference in workplaces — all banned outright under Art. 5.

High-risk uses

Does your app make important decisions?

Credit scoring, hiring, medical diagnosis, education assessment, law enforcement, border control — classified as high-risk with strict requirements.

Compliance gaps

What's missing from your codebase?

Risk management, data governance, logging, transparency, human oversight, accuracy — scored per article with effort estimates.

Documentation

Can you prove compliance?

Auto-generates Annex IV technical documentation from your actual code — functions, dependencies, and logging coverage already populated.

Security

Is your AI code secure?

Checks for prompt injection, unsafe model loading, unvalidated AI output, hardcoded keys, and other AI-specific vulnerabilities.

Transparency

Do your users know it's AI?

Chatbots must disclose they're not human. Deepfakes must be labelled. Regula checks whether your code handles this.

Beyond scanning — evidence you can hand to an auditor

Regula generates structured compliance artefacts from your actual code, not from questionnaires.

New

Conformity assessment evidence

regula conform . generates an Article 43 evidence pack — 26 files mapped to Articles 9-15, per-article readiness scores, SHA-256 integrity hashes. Clearly marks what's auto-generated vs what needs human input.

New

AI Bill of Materials

regula sbom --ai-bom . produces CycloneDX 1.6 AI-BOMs with model provenance extracted from your code, GPAI tier annotations per EU AI Act Art 51-55, and detected training datasets.

New

Human oversight analysis

regula oversight . traces AI model outputs across files and checks whether each path to a user-facing endpoint passes through a human review gate. Reports confidence per path and discloses what it cannot analyse.
