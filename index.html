What the law covers — and what Regula checks

Not a questionnaire. Regula reads your code and maps it to the regulation.

Banned uses

Is your app prohibited?

Social scoring, subliminal manipulation, real-time facial recognition, emotion inference in workplaces — all banned outright under Art. 5.

High-risk uses

Does your app make important decisions?

Credit scoring, hiring, medical diagnosis, education assessment, law enforcement, border control — classified as high-risk with strict requirements.

Compliance gaps

What's missing from your codebase?

Risk management, data governance, logging, transparency, human oversight, accuracy — scored per article with effort estimates.

Documentation

Can you prove compliance?

Auto-generates Annex IV technical documentation from your actual code — functions, dependencies, and logging coverage already populated.

Security

Is your AI code secure?

Checks for prompt injection, unsafe model loading, unvalidated AI output, hardcoded keys, and other AI-specific vulnerabilities.

Transparency

Do your users know it's AI?

Chatbots must disclose they're not human. Deepfakes must be labelled. Regula checks whether your code handles this.
