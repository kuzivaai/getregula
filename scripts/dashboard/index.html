Enforcement Timeline

Key EU AI Act compliance dates.
2 Feb 2025 — Prohibited practices (Article 5) ENFORCED
2 Aug 2025 — GPAI obligations (Articles 51-55) ENFORCED
2 Aug 2026 — High-risk obligations (Annex III) UPCOMING
Omnibus caveat: EP voted 569-45 on 26 Mar 2026 to delay high-risk deadlines to 2 Dec 2027. Trilogue ongoing. Not yet law — Aug 2026 remains the legal baseline.

Audit Trail

SHA-256 hash-chained, tamper-evident scan history.

Every Regula scan is logged in a tamper-evident chain. Verify integrity:

regula audit verify

Optional: anchor to a public RFC 3161 Time Stamp Authority with regula audit anchor --tsa https://freetsa.org/tsr
